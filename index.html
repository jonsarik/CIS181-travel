Welcome to Pictured Rocks National Lakeshore!

Discover the breathtaking beauty of Pictured Rocks National Lakeshore, located in the Upper Peninsula of Michigan. This natural wonder boasts towering multicolored sandstone cliffs, miles of sparkling beaches, dashing waterfalls, and a lush forest. It's a paradise for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts alike.

Whether you're here to hike the scenic trails, kayak along the stunning shoreline, or simply relax and take in the views, Pictured Rocks offers something for everyone. Come and explore this unique destination and create memories that will last a lifetime.
