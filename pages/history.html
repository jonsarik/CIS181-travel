History of Pictured Rocks

Pictured Rocks National Lakeshore, established in 1966, was the first national lakeshore in the United States. Managed by the National Park Service, this 42-mile-long park along Lake Superior features a mix of natural and cultural wonders. Historic sites include an 1874 lighthouse, old Coast Guard buildings, and exhibits on early logging operations in the area.
